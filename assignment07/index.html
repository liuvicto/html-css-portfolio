Final Project Proposal

Topic

This website will be my personal tutoring service for college application reviews, resume/coverletter building, and mentoring.

Audience

High school students who need help with college application and building/revising their resume and coverletter.

Design & Style

The design will be very simple and straightforward. Colors will be very light expect for targeted content, with few words that may potentially confuse the audience. Target content will be in bright orange.

Color Palette

Gray, Dark Gray, White

Inspired by

  • Yelp
  • Spotify

Scope

The website will have 3 pages that includes Home (intro, why, and cost of mentorship), College Application page (brief examples on what I can do and how to sign up), and Resume/Coverletter page. (basic examples and what I will do to help and sign up)

Home
  • Intro
  • The Why and Values
  • Cost of Membership
College Application Page
  • Overview on what I will go over
  • Link to sign up for review
Resume & Coverletter Page
  • Overview on what I will go over
  • Link to sign up for a review
